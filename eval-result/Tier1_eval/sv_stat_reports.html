1. Evaluation results

Variant type match mode: loose (allow type match between DUPLICTION and INSERTION)

The evaluation metrics has two categories after filtering long SV regions: one category is used to highlight performance by metrics including Recall, Precision, and F1 score, and the other category presents benchmark results, which consists of TP_bench, TP_user, FP, FN.
